  Shifting Content
     This example demonstrates a element shifting a few pixels in either direction on each page load.

     1. To load it randomly, append " ?mode=random " to the URL. 

     2. To specify a differant numbor of pixels to shift the example, append " ?pixel_shift=100 " to the URL. 

     3. To do both together, use " ?mode=random&pixel_shift=100 " to the URL.
